The first aha moment creates appetite to continue to reach the next aha moment. You are carving a path to your complete value via these episodes of micro value. PLG offers value dropping across the journey of a product like a page-turning novel.

With value at each step

Imagine, teaching the game of chess to a kid. Nearly everyone of us will start by explaining the rules regarding pawn, king, queen and other. Trying to get the foundational theory out of the way. But the kid on the other hand is looking to have fun and they are just going through a lot of theory without actually getting to play the game.
‍
Now, imagine there is no control on the kid. we will need a way to keep the kid engaged without force. What if there is a way to teach what a pawn can do, and make them play with only pawns. Tell them how can king can move, and play with two kings on the board. It will help in delivering Aha moments and keep the kid engaged.

New habits don’t get forced. They are build by creating value at each step.
